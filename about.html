Origin Story

Our flagship location is based out of Wailuku, Hawai'i. We chose Hawai'i to be our first location because of the impact of asain culture there, specifically Thai. All of our locations are special to each area they are in, but our flagship restaurant in Wailuku, Hawai'i will always be #1 to us as well as the community it thrives in.
